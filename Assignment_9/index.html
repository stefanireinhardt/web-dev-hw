"The Swingin' Sixties"

Known for its vibrant floral patterns and colors, the 1960s is synonymous with the "Flower Child" or Hippie era. The movement arose when Americans became more outspoken in their opposition to the Vietnam War and embraced the flower symbolism to express their desire for peace and non-violence.
