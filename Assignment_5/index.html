Gram's Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 4 Ripe Bananas
  • 11/2 Cups Plain Flower
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together.
  2. Add the eggs, bananas, and vanilla extract. Mix until mixture has little or no lumps.
  3. In a medium bowl, sift flower and mix in baking powder.
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients a little at a time until completely blended.
  5. Pour mixture into a greased loaf pan.
  6. Bake at 360 degrees for *60 minutes.

    7. *Cooking times may very depending on your oven. Insert a toothpick into the bread and when it comes out clean, the bread is done.
